How this demo works

This public prototype runs in the browser. It does not upload or store photos.

It captures a licence frame, captures live frames before and after the biometric moment, attempts a platform biometric prompt through WebAuthn, then reduces the result to a buyer-friendly outcome.

Production use would need hardened identity, biometric, document-fraud, privacy and security architecture.
